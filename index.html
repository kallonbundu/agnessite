WHO WE ARE
WHAT WE DO

“We must defined democracy as that form of government of society which is inspired above every other, with the feeling and consciousness as the dignity of man”.



THOMAS MANN(1875-1955

MISSION

To inform and educate people about democracy, politics, civic rights and responsibility; and to inculcate into their minds the spirit of patriotism: to sustain the reputation of our nation, to uphold her constitution, to maintain her institutions and customs; and to scarifies personal interest for the welfare of the nation by encouraging and enhancing women and young people to play an active and responsible role in the electioneering process through information and services that are reliable, applicable, women centered and sustainable approach


VISION

The hope of a better future which we must all achieve is by enhancing liberal democracy; utilizing our resources and non-human resources and culture to develop a strong economy. In other to achieve our goals; TCFWVID strives to be the best civic education promotion and conflict resolution services delivery agency in Sierra Leone and leading the way to national cohesion; unity and election violence mitigation.


SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

  • To raise high level of public awareness on democracy education and civic right particularly in remote and slum communities; with special interest on first time voters who had very limited practical knowledge of or involvement in the electoral process


  • To encourage and promote women leadership And political tolerance among supports especially women of diver’s political party inclination In the spirit of patriotism and responsible citizenship that will create a positive and sustainable change; and transformation in the whole of Sierra Leone across all themes

  • To train and empower women ,youth facilitators; and grassroots volunteers communicators to disseminate civic information to targeted groups and thereby promotes increased participation in voters registration and voting process in all local and general elections


  • To provide information and services that will enable women and young people to play an active and responsible role in choosing our representative in a patriotic manner


  • To undertake economic development projects such as agriculture, SME, micro finance and develop small industries to empower women and young people to be self-reliance for sustainable change and development of themselves and others.


  • To collaborate with stakeholders and various youths group to speak against election violence and encourage eligible voters to participate peaceful in the electioneering process.


  • To provide psychological aid and counseling in other To build the thinking capacity and changing the behavior of young people who are engage in illegal activities/drug abuse and violent in term of political dispensation


SERVICE DELIVERY PROGRAMS

  1. Advocacy inclusion: human right

  2. Women’s empowerment

  3. Youth development training programs

  4. Community service project

  5. Health support services

  6. Expanding economic opportunities for sustainable development

  7. Peace building
